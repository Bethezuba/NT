01:01 Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God, to the saints who are in Ephesus and those who believe in Him.
01:02 Grace to you and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.
01:03 Blessed is the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who has blessed us spiritually in the heavenly realms in Christ.
01:04 Chosen in Him before the foundation of the world, to be holy and blameless before Him in love.
01:05 Preordained us to adoption as sons through Jesus Christ to Himself, according to the purpose of His will.
01:06 To the praise of His grace, which He has bestowed on us in the beloved.
01:07 In whom we have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of sins, according to the riches of His grace.
01:08 Graced us in all wisdom and insight
01:09 Revealed to us the mystery of His will, according to His purpose which He set forth in Christ
01:10 In Him, to sum up all things in heaven and on earth, according to the purpose of the one who accomplishes all things.
01:11 In whom we have also been chosen according to His purpose, who accomplishes all things according to the counsel of His will.
01:12 In whom we have obtained an inheritance to the praise of His glory, to those who were predestined in Christ.
01:13 In whom you also, after hearing the word of truth, the gospel of your salvation, in whom believing you were sealed with the Holy Spirit of promise.
01:14 The deposit of our inheritance, for the redemption of the possession, to the praise of His glory.
01:15 Through this, I also, having heard the faith in you toward the Lord Jesus and the love for all the saints,
01:16 I do not cease to give thanks for you, making mention of you in my prayers.
01:17 Grants you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of Him.
01:18 Enlightened are the eyes of your hearts to understand what is the hope of His calling, and what is the riches of the glory of His inheritance among the saints.
01:19 And what is the exceeding greatness of His power toward us who believe, according to the working of His mighty strength.
01:20 Raised Him from the dead and seated Him at His right hand in the heavenly places.
01:21 Above every name that is named, not only in this age but also in the one to come.
01:22 And he put all things under his feet, and gave him head over all things to the church.
01:23 This is his body, the fullness of him who fills all in all.
02:01 Dead in your transgressions and sins
02:02 In which you once walked according to the course of this world, according to the ruler of the authority of the air, the spirit now working in the sons of disobedience.
02:03 In whom we also once lived in the desires of our flesh, following the course of this world, following the ruler of the authority of the air, the spirit that is now at work in those who are disobedient. And we were by nature children of wrath, even as the rest.
02:04 Rich in mercy, because of His great love with which He loved us
02:05 And He made us alive together with Christ, by grace you have been saved.
02:06 Raised us up together, and seated us together in the heavenly places in Christ Jesus.
02:07 In the coming ages, he might show the immeasurable riches of his grace in kindness toward us in Christ Jesus.
02:08 You are saved by grace through faith. And this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God.
02:09 Not from works, lest anyone should boast.
02:10 We are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus, for good works which God prepared beforehand, that we should walk in them.
02:11 You were Gentiles, hastily brought to Christ by the circumcision of Christ.
02:12 You were at that time without Christ, aliens from the commonwealth of Israel and strangers to the covenants of promise, devoid of hope and godless in the world.
02:13 Now in Christ Jesus, you who were far off have been brought near by the blood of Christ.
02:14 He is our peace, who has made both one and broke down the middle wall of separation, having abolished in His flesh the enmity.
02:15 Abolishing in His ordinances the law of commandments, to create in Himself two into one new man, making peace.
02:16 And in Him, through the Cross, He reconciled both to God, by wiping out the enmity.
02:17 And coming, He proclaimed peace to you who are far off and peace to those who are near.
02:18 Through Him we have access by one Spirit to the Father.
02:19 Therefore you are no longer outsiders or strangers, but fellow citizens with the saints and members of God's household.
02:20 Built upon the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Jesus Christ being the chief cornerstone.
02:21 In Him all the building, being joined together, grows into a holy temple in the Lord.
02:22 In Him, you also are being built together into a dwelling place of God in the Spirit.
03:01 For this reason I, Paul, the prisoner of Christ Jesus for you Gentiles,
03:02 If you have heard the administration of the grace of God which was given to me for you,
03:03 Revealed to me is the mystery, as I wrote briefly
03:04 Understand my insight in the mystery of Christ
03:05 Revealed to the sons of men as it is now revealed to the holy apostles and prophets in spirit.
03:06 Belonging to the gentiles are fellow heirs, members of the same body, and fellow partakers of the promise in Christ Jesus through the gospel.
03:07 According to the grace given me by God, I am a servant of that grace.
03:08 To me, who am less than all the saints, was this grace given— to preach to the Gentiles the unsearchable riches of Christ.
03:09 And to enlighten all what is the administration of the mystery hidden in God, who created all things, This is the English translation for the Greek text from Ephesians 3:9. As per your request, I've removed any additional context or explanatory elements.
03:10 The wisdom of God, in its rich variety, is made known to the rulers and authorities in the heavenly realms through the church.
03:11 According to the purpose of the ages, which He (God) accomplished in Christ Jesus our Lord,
03:12 In whom we have boldness and access with confidence through faith in Him.
03:13 I entreat you not to grieve me over my tribulations for your sake, which is your glory.
03:14 For this reason I bow my knees to the Father
03:15 All fatherhood in heaven and on earth is named after you
03:16 Empower you, by the power that is from his wealth of glory, to be strengthened within the inner person, by means of His Spirit.
03:17 Inhabit Christ through faith in your hearts, love. Rooted and established.
03:18 Reach the full extent of your understanding, together with all the saints.
03:19 Understand the surpassing knowledge of Christ's love, to reach the measure of all the fullness of God.
03:20 According to His mighty power, which He worked in us, He is able to accomplish far more than all we can ask or imagine.
03:21 Glory to Him in the church and in Christ Jesus throughout all ages, world without end. Amen.
04:01 I urge you, therefore, as a fellow prisoner in the Lord, to walk worthy of the calling with which you were called.
04:02 With all humility and gentleness, bearing with one another in love
04:03 Maintaining the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace
04:04 One body and one Spirit, even as you were called in one hope of your calling.
04:05 One Lord, one faith, one baptism
04:06 One God and Father of all, who is over all and through all and in all.
04:07 Each of us has received grace according to the measure of Christ's gift.
04:08 Raised to the highest point, he made captive the captives, and gave gifts to people.
04:09 Went down what is it, if not that also descended into the lower parts of the earth?
04:10 He who descended is also the one who ascended above all the heavens, in order to fill all things.
04:11 And He Himself gave some as apostles, others as prophets, others as evangelists, and others as shepherds and teachers.
04:12 For the perfecting of the saints for the work of service, to the building up of the body of Christ
04:13 We will all attain to the unity of the faith and of the knowledge of the Son of God, to a mature man, to the measure of the stature of the fullness of Christ.
04:14 Not children any longer, we should not be tossed to and fro by every teaching, by the cunning craftiness of men in deceitful scheming.
04:15 Speaking the truth in love, we are to grow to him who is the head, Christ.
04:16 Builds up itself in love, as each part does its work.
04:17 This says and testifies in the Lord that you should no longer walk as the Gentiles do, in the futility of their minds.
04:18 Darkened in understanding, alienated from the life of God, because of the ignorance that is in them, because of the hardness of their hearts.
04:19 Yielding to the flesh, they gave themselves over to impurity for every kind of greed.
04:20 You did not learn Christ in this way
04:21 If indeed you heard and in Him were taught, as truth is in Jesus
04:22 Lay aside the former way of life, the old man who is being corrupted according to deceitful desires;
04:23 Renew according to the spirit of your mind
04:24 And put on the new self, created according to God in righteousness and holiness of truth.
04:25 Speak truth each one with his neighbor, for we are members of one another.
04:26 Anger not, nor sin; let not the sun go down upon your wrath.
04:27 Do not give place to the devil.
04:28 Steal no more, but rather work, making something good with your own hands, so that you may have something to share with the one in need.
04:29 Every foul language out of your mouth should be stopped, but that which is good for building up, give grace to those who hear.
04:30 Do not grieve the Holy Spirit of God, in whom you were sealed for the day of redemption.
04:31 All bitterness, anger, wrath, clamor, and slander be put away from you, together with all malice.
04:32 Become to one another compassionate, kind, forgiving each other, as God in Christ also has forgiven you.
05:01 Be imitators of God, as beloved children.
05:02 Walk in love, as Christ also loved us and gave Himself up for us as an offering and a sacrifice to God as a fragrant aroma.
05:03 Immorality or impurity of any kind, or greed, should not be mentioned among you, as is fitting for saints.
05:04 Shamelessness, foolish talking, or obscenity—these things are not becoming of saints; rather, give thanks.
05:05 This means: "Know this for certain, that the immoral, impure, or greedy (idolaters), have no inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and God."
05:06 Does not anyone deceive you with empty words, for wrath of God comes upon the children of disobedience.
05:07 Do not be partakers with them.
05:08 Were once darkness, now light in the Lord. As children of light, walk.
05:09 Fruit of light consists in all goodness, righteousness, and truth.
05:10 Testing what is pleasing to the Lord
05:11 Do not participate in the unfruitful works of darkness, but rather expose them.
05:12 Unfitting things are done in secret by them, and it is shameful to speak of.
05:13 All things are revealed by the light.
05:14 All that is revealed is light. Therefore it says, "Awake, you who sleep, and arise from the dead, and Christ will shine on you."
05:15 Walk wisely, with insight. Not as unwise but as wise.
05:16 Dark days are coming, so be alert.
05:17 Understand, therefore, be not foolish, but wise, recognizing what is the will of the Lord.
05:18 Do not get drunk on wine, within which is dissipation, but be filled in the Spirit.
05:19 Speaking to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and playing to the heart of each one of you to the Lord.
05:20 Thanking always for all things in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ to God and Father.
05:21 Submit to one another out of reverence for Christ.
05:22 Wives, submit to your own husbands as to the Lord.
05:23 Husband is the head of the wife as also Christ is the head of the church, himself the savior of the body.
05:24 Wives, submit to your husbands as to the Lord.
05:25 Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church and gave himself up for her
05:26 Sanctify her, having cleansed her by the washing of water in the word.
05:27 The church should be presented to herself as glorious, without spot or wrinkle or any such thing, but that she may be holy and without fault.
05:28 Thus husbands should love their wives as their own bodies; he who loves his wife loves himself.
05:29 Nobody hates his own flesh, but nourishes and cherishes it, just as Christ also the church.
05:30 We are members of His body.
05:31 Leaves his father and mother, and is joined to his wife, and the two shall become one flesh.
05:32 This mystery is great; yet I speak concerning Christ and the church.
05:33 However, each of you should love his own wife as himself, and the wife should respect her husband.
06:01 Obey your parents in the Lord, for this is right.
06:02 Honor your father and mother, which is the first commandment with promise.
06:03 May it be well with you, and you may live long on the earth.
06:04 Fathers, do not provoke your children to anger, but nurture them in the discipline and instruction of the Lord.
06:05 Submit to your human masters with fear and trembling, in sincerity of heart as to Christ
06:06 Not in servility as people-pleasers, but as Christ's servants doing God's will, from the heart.
06:07 Serving with grace, as to the Lord and not to people
06:08 Recognizing that whoever does what is good, this will be received from the Lord, whether slave or free.
06:09 And the Lord treats you equally, not respecting persons, knowing that both theirs and yours is the Lord in heaven, and there is no partiality with Him.
06:10 In the Lord's strength, you are able.
06:11 Put on the armor of God, so that you may be able to stand against the schemes of the devil.
06:12 Against the rulers, against the authorities, against the cosmic powers of this darkness, against the spiritual forces of evil in the heavenly places.
06:13 Take up the armor of God, so that you may be able to stand against the evil day, and after fighting, to have stood firm.
06:14 Stand firm then, with truth as the belt around your waist. And put on the breastplate of righteousness.
06:15 With the preparation of the Gospel of peace, put on your feet
06:16 With the shield of faith, you can extinguish all the fiery darts of the wicked.
06:17 Accept the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.
06:18 Through all prayer and supplication, praying at all times in the Spirit, and watch, with all perseverance and supplication, for all the saints.
06:19 And for me, that word may be given to me in opening the mouth of my boldness, to make known the mystery of the gospel.
06:20 For whom I am an ambassador, in bonds, that I may boldly speak as I ought to speak.
06:21 You yourselves know, that which I am doing, all will be made known to you by Tychicus, the beloved brother and faithful servant in the Lord.
06:22 This one I sent to you for this purpose, that you may know things concerning us and admonish your hearts.
06:23 Peace to the brothers, and love with faith from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.
06:24 Grace to all who love our Lord Jesus Christ forever.
