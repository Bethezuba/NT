01:01 Dear lady and children, whom I love in truth, and not I only, but also all who know the truth
01:02 In truth that remains in us, and will be with us forever.
01:03 Grace, mercy, peace from God the Father and from Jesus Christ the Son of the Father, in truth and love.
01:04 I have greatly rejoiced that I found some of your children walking in truth, just as we received a commandment from the Father.
01:05 And now I entreat you, lady, not as commanding a new one to you, but that which we had from the beginning, so that we love one another.
01:06 And this is love, that we walk according to his commands. This is the commandment, as you have heard from the beginning, that you abide in it.
01:07 Many have departed into the world who do not confess Jesus Christ coming in flesh. This is the deception and the antichrist.
01:08 Observe yourselves, that you may not lose what we have achieved, but may gain a full reward.
01:09 Everybody who continues in the teaching of Christ has God. The one who continues in the teaching, he also has the Father and the Son.
01:10 Does not bring this teaching — do not receive him into your house, nor greet him.
01:11 He who says he is coming and brings this teaching, has part in his wicked works.
01:12 I had many things to write to you, but I did not want to do so with paper and ink. Instead, I hope to visit you and talk face-to-face, so that your joy may be made complete.
01:13 Greets you the children of your chosen sister.
