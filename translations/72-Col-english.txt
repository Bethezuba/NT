01:01 Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, and Timothy, the brother.
01:02 To the saints and faithful brothers in Christ at Colossae: Grace to you and peace from God our Father.
01:03 We give thanks to God the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ always, praying for you
01:04 Hearing the faith in Christ Jesus and the love which you have for all saints
01:05 The hope which is laid up for you in heaven, of which you heard before in the word of truth of the Gospel.
01:06 The present that is destined for you, as it is also in the whole world producing fruit and growing, so it is also among you, from which day you heard and knew the grace of God in truth.
01:07 According to Epaphras, our beloved fellow servant, who is faithful on our behalf as a minister of Christ
01:08 Revealed to us your love in the Spirit.
01:09 We do not cease to pray for you, asking that you may be filled with the knowledge of His will in all wisdom and spiritual understanding.
01:10 Walk worthy of the Lord, fully pleasing to Him, bearing fruit in every good work and increasing in the knowledge of God.
01:11 In all power, being strengthened according to the might of His glory, with endurance and patience, with joy.
01:12 Thanking the Father who qualified you for a share in the inheritance of the saints in the light
01:13 Translated: Who rescued us from the dominion of darkness and transferred us to the kingdom of the Son of His love.
01:14 In whom we have redemption, the forgiveness of sins.
01:15 Image of the invisible God, firstborn of all creation
01:16 In him all things were created, both in heaven and on earth, visible and invisible, whether thrones or dominions or rulers or authorities—all things were created through him and for him.
01:17 He is before all and in him all things hold together.
01:18 He is the head of the body, the church; who is the beginning, the firstborn from the dead, that in all things he might have the preeminence.
01:19 All fullness dwells in him.
01:20 Through Him, He reconciles all things to Himself, making peace through the blood of His cross, whether on earth or in heaven.
01:21 And you, who at one time were estranged and enemies in your minds because of your evil actions.
01:22 Now you have been reconciled in his body of flesh by death, presenting you holy, and blameless, and unreproveable in his sight.
01:23 If you continue in the faith, grounded and steadfast, and not shifting from the hope of the gospel which you heard, which was proclaimed in all creation under heaven, of which I became a servant, Paul.
01:24 Now I rejoice in my sufferings for you, and I complete what is lacking in Christ's afflictions in his body on behalf of the church.
01:25 I became a servant of the administration of God, which was given to me for you, to fulfill the word of God.
01:26 The mystery hidden from ages and generations has now been revealed to his saints.
01:27 Which God willed to make known among the Gentiles is the gospel of Christ, the riches of glory of this mystery, which is Christ in you, the hope of glory.
01:28 We are proclaiming and urging all people to live a life worthy of God, who calls you into His own kingdom and glory. Teaching them with all wisdom so that we may present every person mature in Christ.
01:29 Striving according to His power which is at work within me.
02:01 I have a struggle on your behalf, and for those in Laodicea, and for all who have not seen my face in the flesh.
02:02 That they may be encouraged, being knit together in love, attaining to the knowledge of the mystery of God, Christ.
02:03 All the treasures of wisdom and knowledge are hidden in Him.
02:04 This translates to: "That no one may boast in your presence."
02:05 I am with you in spirit, rejoicing and seeing your order and the firmness of your faith in Christ.
02:06 In Him walk.
02:07 Rooted and built up in Him, and established in the faith, just as you were taught, overflowing with gratitude.
02:08 Do not be carried away by diverse and opposing ideas, which result from human tradition rather than from Christ.
02:09 In him dwells all the fullness of the godhead bodily.
02:10 You are complete in him, who is the head of all rule and authority.
02:11 Circumcised in him was your circumcision made without hands in the taking off of the body of the flesh, in the circumcision of Christ.
02:12 In him you were also raised with him, through faith in the working of God, who raised him from the dead.
02:13 And you, who were once dead in your transgressions and the uncircumcision of your flesh, He made alive together with him. Grace be to all of us, and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.
02:14 Erased the handwriting that was against us, which was opposing us, and He disarmed it from the cross.
02:15 Disarmed the authorities and the powers, He displayed them openly, triumphing over them in Him.
02:16 Do not let anyone judge you in food or in drink, or regarding a festival or a new moon or Sabbaths.
02:17 The shadow of things to come; but the substance is of Christ.
02:18 No one must intimidate you with empty words based on human philosophy and the religion of angels, which he claims to have seen in visions, being merely a reflection of the fleshly nature of his own mind.
02:19 And he is not holding the head, from which all the body by means of joints and bands grows with a growth of God.
02:20 If you have died with Christ, why are you being made subject to rules as if you were still in the world?
02:21 Do not touch, do not taste, do not handle
02:22 Everything that perishes with the use, according to human commandments and teachings?
02:23 Wisdom, with understanding in will-worship, humility, and unwavering endurance of the body, is not acceptable to the flesh for its gratification.
03:01 If then you were raised with Christ, seek the things that are above, where Christ is seated at the right hand of God.
03:02 Consider the things that are above, not the things on earth.
03:03 Have died, indeed, and your life hidden with Christ in God.
03:04 When Christ appears, then you also will appear with Him in glory.
03:05 Mortify therefore the members which are upon the earth: fornication, uncleanness, passion, evil desire, and covetousness, which is idolatry.
03:06 God's wrath comes upon the children of disobedience.
03:07 In whom you also once lived (The Greek phrase is: "In quibus et vos periepatesate pothete, hoc est, in his ubi quaeratis.")
03:08 Discard all (things)—anger, wrath, malice, slander, and obscene talk from your mouth.
03:09 Do not lie to one another. Putting off the old self with its practices.
03:10 Put on the new self, created to be like God in true righteousness and holiness.
03:11 In all and in each, Christ.
03:12 Clothe yourselves with compassion, kindness, humility, gentleness, and patience. Bear with each other and forgive one another if any of you has a grievance against someone. Forgive as the Lord forgave you. And over all these virtues put on love, which binds them all together in perfect unity. However, for your specific request, the Greek phrase " , , , , , , " translates to: "Clothe yourselves with the attributes of God's chosen ones, being holy and loving, compassionate, kind, humble, gentle, patient, bearing with one another and forgiving each other."
03:13 Forgive each other, just as the Lord forgave you.
03:14 Upon these things, love; for it is the bond of perfection.
03:15 And the peace of Christ be with you, which also ye were called in one body: and be ye thankful.
03:16 The Word of Christ dwells in you richly, in every wisdom. Teaching and admonishing one another in spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to God.
03:17 In the name of Jesus Christ, give thanks to the Father.
03:18 Women, submit to your husbands as is fitting in the Lord.
03:19 Men, love your wives and do not be bitter towards them.
03:20 Children, obey your parents in everything, for this is acceptable in the Lord.
03:21 Fathers, do not provoke your children, lest they become disheartened.
03:22 Obey in all things the earthly masters, not in service to human passions, but with simplicity of heart, fearing the Lord.
03:23 Whatever you do, do from the heart, as to the Lord and not to men.
03:24 Receiving the reward of the inheritance from the Lord, serve the Lord Christ.
03:25 The wrongdoer will receive what they deserve, and there is no room for partiality.
04:01 Lords, the righteousness and equality are given to the slaves, knowing that you also have a Lord in heaven.
04:02 Pray in it, being vigilant in it with thankfulness
04:03 Pray for us also, that God may open to us the door of faith, and reveal the mystery of Christ, in whom I am bound.
04:04 Reveal it to me, as I ought to speak.
04:05 Walk wisely towards outsiders, redeeming the time.
04:06 Your message in English is: "Your word in everything should be in grace, seasoned with salt, to know how each one of you ought to answer."
04:07 Tychicus, the beloved brother and faithful servant in the Lord, will make known to you all things that concern me.
04:08 Knowing the things concerning us, and admonishing you to have a change of heart
04:09 With Onesimus the faithful and beloved brother, who is among you, all these things will be made known to you.
04:10 Greets you Aristarchus, my fellow-prisoner, and Mark the cousin of Barnabas (whom you received commands about, if he comes to you, receive him).
04:11 And Jesus, who is called Justus, who are of the circumcision, these only are fellow workers for the kingdom of God, who have been comfort to me.
04:12 Greets you Epaphras, who is from among you, a servant of Christ. Always laboring on your behalf in prayers, that you may stand perfect and fully knowing the will of God.
04:13 He has a great labor on your behalf, and for Laodiceans and those in Hierapolis.
04:14 Greets you Luke, the beloved physician, and Demas.
04:15 Greet the brothers in Laodicea, Nympha and the church in her house.
04:16 And when the epistle is read among you, let it also be read in the Laodicean church, and that from Laodicea let it be read by you.
04:17 Speak to Archippus: "Behold the service which you received in the Lord, fulfill it."
04:18 Greetings from me, Paul. Remember my chains. Grace be with you.
