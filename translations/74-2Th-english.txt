01:01 Paul, Silas, and Timothy, to the church of the Thessalonians in God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ:
01:02 Grace to you and peace from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.
01:03 We ought to thank God always concerning you, brethren, as is fitting, because your faith grows and love increases among all of you towards one another.
01:04 We take pride in you in the churches of God for your endurance and faith in all your persecutions and tribulations which you are bearing.
01:05 Example of righteous judgment of God, to be considered worthy of the kingdom of God, for which you are also suffering.
01:06 Repaying accordingly to God in your case, suffering to those who are suffering.
01:07 Peace to you who are suffering, from us by the revelation of the Lord Jesus Christ from heaven with the angels of His power.
01:08 In the flaming fire, giving retribution to those who do not know God and to those who do not obey the gospel of our Lord Jesus.
01:09 Those who bring destruction upon themselves from the presence of the Lord and from the glory of His power.
01:10 When he comes to be glorified in his saints and marveled at by all who believe, that our testimony may be established in you on that day.
01:11 We are praying for you continually, so that our God may deem you worthy of your calling, and fulfill every desire of goodness and the work of faith in power.
01:12 That the name of our Lord Jesus may be glorified in you, and you in Him, according to the grace of our God and the Lord Jesus Christ.
02:01 We ask you, brothers, concerning the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ and our gathering together to Him
02:02 Not to be quickly shaken in mind or alarmed, either by a spirit or an emissary or a letter as from us, as though the day of the Lord has come.
02:03 Does not anyone deceive you in any way; for the falling away (apostasy) must come first, and the man of lawlessness be revealed, the son of destruction.
02:04 Opponent and usurper, claiming to be something called God or divinity, sits in the temple of God, demonstrating that he himself is God.
02:05 Do you not remember that while I was still with you, I told you these things?
02:06 Now the one holding back will be revealed in his own time.
02:07 The mystery of lawlessness is already working, only he who now restrains will do so until he is taken out of the way.
02:08 And then the lawless one will be revealed, whom the Lord Jesus will kill with the breath of his mouth and destroy by the appearance of his coming.
02:09 The presence is of the Satan in all power, signs, and lying wonders
02:10 And in every deception to those perishing, among whom the love of the truth was not found that they might be saved.
02:11 Sends them the God deception for believing the lie
02:12 All who did not believe the truth but took pleasure in wickedness.
02:13 We ought to give thanks to God always for you, brothers loved by the Lord, because God chose you as firstfruits for salvation through sanctification by the Spirit and faith in the truth.
02:14 For whom He called you through our gospel, to the attainment of glory of our Lord Jesus Christ.
02:15 Therefore, brothers, stand firm and hold to the traditions that you were taught by us, either by word of mouth or by letter.
02:16 Our Lord Jesus Christ and God, the Father of us, who loves us and gives us eternal comfort and a good hope in grace.
02:17 Encourage your hearts and establish you in every good word and work.
03:01 Pray for us, brethren, that the word of the Lord may run and be glorified, just as also among you.
03:02 And that we may be delivered from wicked and evil men, for not all have faith.
03:03 Faithful is the Lord, who will sustain and protect you from evil.
03:04 We trust in the Lord concerning you, that you do what we command and will do so.
03:05 The Lord directs your hearts toward the love of God and the endurance of Christ.
03:06 We command you, brothers, in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, that you withdraw from every brother who walks disorderly and not according to the tradition which we received from him.
03:07 You yourselves know how you ought to follow us, for we did not act unwisely among you
03:08 We did not eat anyone's bread for free, but in toil and hardship, night and day, we worked to avoid being a burden to any of you.
03:09 We do not have authority, but to make ourselves an example for you to follow.
03:10 We commanded you when we were with you that if anyone does not want to work, he should not eat.
03:11 We hear of some walking among you in disorderly manner, working not but busybodies.
03:12 We command and entreat those of this sort in the Lord Jesus Christ that with quietness they work, and eat their own bread.
03:13 You, brethren, do not grow weary in doing good.
03:14 Ignore this person if he does not listen to our word through the letter, so that he may be ashamed.
03:15 Do not regard him as an enemy, but admonish him as a brother.
03:16 The Lord of peace himself give you peace at all times in every way. The Lord be with all of you.
03:17 Greetings from me, Paul's hand, which is a signature in every letter; thus I write.
03:18 The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all.
