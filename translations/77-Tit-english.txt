01:01 Paul, a servant of God, an apostle of Jesus Christ according to the faith of God, and knowledge of the truth that is according to godliness:
01:02 In hope of eternal life, which God, who does not lie, promised long ages ago
01:03 Revealed at certain times, his message which I proclaimed according to the commandment of our Savior God.
01:04 Dear Titus, genuine child in our common faith; Grace and peace from God the Father and our Savior Jesus Christ.
01:05 You left in Crete to set things in order, and to appoint elders according to your instruction, as I directed you.
01:06 An unblamable man, the husband of one wife, having faithful children, not accused of dissipation or rebellion.
01:07 The overseer must be blameless, as a steward of God; not arrogant, not angry, not greedy; not drunken, not violent, not greedy for sordid gain.
01:08 Hospitalitable, loving what is good, self-controlled, just, holy, self-disciplined.
01:09 Enduring the teaching that is in accord with faith, so that he may also exhort in sound doctrine and rebuke those who contradict.
01:10 Unruly are many, ungovernable, vain talkers, and deceivers—especially those of the circumcision.
01:11 These are to be silenced, who overthrow houses, teaching things that ought not for shameful gain.
01:12 Liars, ever, Cretans, evil beasts, lazy bellies.
01:13 This testimony is true. For this reason I reproved them sharply, so that they might be sound in the faith.
01:14 Not adhering to Jewish myths and the commands of those who turn away from the truth.
01:15 All things are pure to the pure, but to those who are defiled and unbelieving nothing is pure; but both their mind and conscience are defiled.
01:16 Confessing God, they are known; but to their works they deny, being defiled and unbelieving, and to every good work they are disapproved.
02:01 You, however, speak as you should to the healthy teaching.
02:02 Wise elders, reverent, sensible, healthy in faith, love, and endurance.
02:03 Respectable elders, living as becomes the gospel, not being under the influence of Satan or heavy drinking, teachers who are worthy of respect.
02:04 Nurture the young women to be sensible, loving husbands, and loving mothers.
02:05 Obedient, pure, diligent, good, submitting to their own husbands, so that the word of God may not be blasphemed.
02:06 Admonish the younger ones to be sober-minded
02:07 Displaying the English translation: Regarding all things, show yourself an example of good works in doctrine, in dignity.
02:08 Healthy discourse, unfathomable, so that the one from the opposing side, having nothing to say about us, may not speak ill of us.
02:09 Submitters to their own masters, to be well-pleasing, not contrary.
02:10 Not nitpicking, but demonstrating good faith with a sound doctrine, so that the teaching of our savior God may be adorned in all things.
02:11 Appeared indeed the grace of God, savior for all people.
02:12 Training us to renounce ungodliness and worldly passions, to live self-controlled, righteous, and devout in this present age.
02:13 Receiving the blessed hope and the glory of the great God and Savior Jesus Christ
02:14 Redeemed us from all lawlessness, and purified for himself a people for his own possession who are zealous for good works.
02:15 Admonishes and exhorts and reproves with all commandment—no one among you should be haughty.
03:01 Submit yourselves to the authorities, be obedient, be ready for every good work.
03:02 Do not blaspheme, be harmless, gentle, show courtesy to all people.
03:03 Were once foolish, disobedient, deceived, serving various lusts and pleasures, living in malice and envy, hateful, hating one another.
03:04 When kindness and love from our savior God appeared
03:05 Saved us through the laundry of regeneration and renewal by the Holy Spirit.
03:06 Richly poured out upon us through Jesus Christ, our Savior.
03:07 Become heirs according to his grace, through hope of eternal life.
03:08 Faithful is the message, and concerning these things I want you to affirm, so that those who have believed God may be careful to engage in good works. These things are good and beneficial for people.
03:09 Seeks foolish disputes, genealogies, and strife over the law, these are senseless and vain.
03:10 Rebuke a divisive person
03:11 Aware that such a person has been overthrown and is sinning, being self-condemned.
03:12 Send Artemas or Tychicus to you, and have them hurry to me in Nicopolis; for I have decided there to spend the winter.
03:13 Obey the civil authorities, and Apollos diligently send on; that nothing may be wanting to them.
03:14 Learn also, that our people should be prepared to do good deeds to meet their necessary needs, so that they are not unfruitful.
03:15 Greetings to you from all those who are with me. Greet the brethren loving us in faith. Grace be with you all.
