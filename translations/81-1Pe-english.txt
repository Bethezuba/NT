01:01 Peter, an apostle of Jesus Christ, to the elect exiles of the Dispersion in Pontus, Galatia, Cappadocia, Asia, and Bithynia.
01:02 According to the knowledge of God, the Father, in the sanctification of the Spirit, for obedience and a sincere conscience toward Jesus Christ: Grace be unto you and peace multiplied.
01:03 Blessed is the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who according to His great mercy has caused us to be born again to a living hope through the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead.
01:04 Inheritance imperishable, undefiled, and unfading, kept in heaven for you
01:05 Faithful through grace, ready to be revealed in the last time.
01:06 Rejoice in this, if for a little while you have suffered grief in various trials.
01:07 So that the testing of your faith, being more precious than gold that perishes though tested by fire, may be found to result in praise and glory and honor at the revelation of Jesus Christ.
01:08 Loving one whom you have not seen, in whom, though now not seeing, you believe and rejoice with an indescribable and glorious joy,
01:09 Bringing forth the outcome of your faith, salvation for souls.
01:10 Regarding which salvation prophets who prophesied concerning the grace that would be yours have inquired and searched intensely
01:11 Investigating into whom or when the Spirit of Christ in them, which testifies beforehand concerning the sufferings that are yours in Christ and the glory that will follow, has made known.
01:12 Revealed to those who have inquired, that not to themselves do they serve these things, but now announced to you by those proclaiming the gospel sent from heaven, for which angels long to peer.
01:13 Raising up the deceased thoughts of your minds, complete your hope towards the grace that is coming to you in the revelation of Jesus Christ.
01:14 As obedient children, do not conform to the former desires of your ignorance.
01:15 Holy as He who called you, be holy in all your conduct.
01:16 Holy, for it is written: "You shall be holy, for I am holy."
01:17 Fear in the presence of Him who judges impartially according to each one's deeds, your time as foreigners spend it in reverence.
01:18 You were redeemed with imperishable precious blood, like that of a lamb, from your futile ways inherited from your forefathers.
01:19 Pure, precious blood of Christ
01:20 Predestined before the foundation of the world, revealed at the end of time through you
01:21 Faith and hope are in God for those who raised him from the dead and gave glory to him, so that your faith and hope may be in God.
01:22 Love one another fervently from the heart, in sincere compassion.
01:23 Born not of perishable seed but of imperishable, through the living and enduring word of God.
01:24 All flesh is like grass, and all its glory like the flower of grass. The grass withers, and the flower falls off.
01:25 The word of the Lord endures forever. This is the gospel that was preached to you.
02:01 Discard all wickedness, deceit, hypocrisy, envy, slander, and every evil speech.
02:02 Yearn for the pure, unfathomable milk of the word, that by it you may grow in salvation.
02:03 The Lord is kind.
02:04 Approaching him, a living stone, rejected by humans but chosen and precious in God's sight.
02:05 You yourselves, like living stones, are being built into a spiritual house, to be a holy priesthood, offering spiritual sacrifices acceptable to God through Jesus Christ.
02:06 Built upon a foundation of apostles and prophets, Jesus Christ Himself being the chief cornerstone; whoever believes in Him will not be put to shame.
02:07 You are honor to those who believe: the stone which builders rejected has become the head of the corner.
02:08 Rejected stone, but a stumbling stone and a rock of offense— they trip over the word, being disobedient to it; to this they were also appointed.
02:09 Chosen generation, royal priesthood, holy nation, people for God's own possession, that you may proclaim the excellencies of Him who called you out of darkness into His marvelous light.
02:10 Once not a people, now you are the people of God; once not mercied, now mercied.
02:11 Beloved, I urge you as aliens and strangers to abstain from fleshly desires, which wage war against the soul.
02:12 Maintain a good behavior among the nations, so that in your good works they may observe God, on the day of inspection.
02:13 Submit yourselves to every ordinance of man for the Lord's sake: whether it be to kings, as supreme;
02:14 Obedience to rulers as sent by Him for punishment of evildoers, praise for those who do good.
02:15 Therefore, the will of God is that the good should be silent concerning the ignorance of foolish men.
02:16 As free, not using your freedom as a cover-up for evil, but living as servants of God.
02:17 Honor all, love the brotherhood, fear God, honor the King.
02:18 Submitters, in every fear to masters, not only to the good and gentle but also to the harsh.
02:19 This is gracious if someone endures grief, suffering unjustly for the sake of God's approval.
02:20 If while sinning and being mistreated, you endure; but if while doing good and suffering, you endure, this is favor with God.
02:21 For this reason, you were called, because Christ also suffered for you, leaving an example for you to follow in His steps.
02:22 A man who has never sinned and was found without deceit in his mouth.
02:23 Condemned, he did not revile; suffering, he did not threaten; but gave himself up for the one who condemns justly.
02:24 He himself bore our sins in his body on the cross, so that we might die to sin and live for righteousness; by whose wounds you have been healed.
02:25 You were like wandering sheep, but now turn to the Shepherd and Overseer of your souls.
03:01 Wives, submit yourselves to your own husbands, so that even if some do not obey the word, they may be won without a word by the behavior of their wives.
03:02 Observing a reverent and holy conduct in your lives.
03:03 External adornment is not changing your inner self. Rather, let it be the unfading beauty of a gentle and quiet spirit, which is of great worth in God's sight.
03:04 A hidden person of the heart, in the unfading nature of a gentle and quiet spirit, is precious in God's sight.
03:05 Thus also the holy women who hoped in God adorned themselves, submitting to their own husbands.
03:06 As Sarah obeyed Abraham, calling him lord: whom you have become the offspring who are doing good and not being fearful of any threat.
03:07 Men likewise dwelling together, consider it a trustworthy thing with reference to your sister wives, that you exercise courtesy towards them as to weak vessels, even as also joint heirs of the grace of life, so that your prayers may not be hindered.
03:08 All of you have unity, sympathize, love as brothers, be compassionate, humble.
03:09 Do not repay evil for evil or abuse for abuse; but, on the contrary, bless, because to this you were called so that you may inherit a blessing.
03:10 Cease from evil and speaking deceitfully,
03:11 Turn away from evil and do good. Seek peace and pursue it.
03:12 The eyes of the Lord are on the righteous, And his ears are open to their cry. But the face of the Lord is against those who do evil.
03:13 Who shall harm you if you become imitators of what is good?
03:14 Blessed are you if you suffer for righteousness; do not be afraid of their threats nor be troubled.
03:15 Sanctify Christ the Lord in your hearts, always ready to make a defense to everyone who asks you a reason concerning the hope that is in you.
03:16 With sincerity and reverence, having a good conscience, so that those who revile your good conduct in Christ may be ashamed.
03:17 Better to suffer for doing good, if that be the will of God, than to do evil.
03:18 Christ also suffered once for sins, righteous for the unrighteous, that He might bring you to God, having been put to death in flesh but made alive by the Spirit.
03:19 In which also he went preaching to the spirits in prison.
03:20 Disobeyed once when the patience of God was waiting in the days of Noah, during the construction of the ark, into which eight souls were saved through water.
03:21 This Greek text translates to: "Likewise, baptism now saves you—not the removal of dirt from the flesh, but an appeal to God for a good conscience—through the resurrection of Jesus Christ."
03:22 He who is going to heaven, having been at God's right hand, with angels, authorities, and powers subject to him.
04:01 Christ's suffering in flesh, and you as well arm yourselves with the same attitude, for the one who has suffered in flesh has put an end to sin.
04:02 Live the rest of your time in the flesh no longer for human desires, but in the will of God.
04:03 Diligent, for the time that remains is short; therefore it is necessary that you should be self-controlled, and that you should not indulge in unrestrained lusts like those that plunge others into dissipation, sensuality, drunkenness, orgies, carousing, and various forms of idolatry.
04:04 Abstain from participation in the same licentious acts that they do, for it is right to have an understanding that such people will receive the due penalty of their error.
04:05 They have received the sentence that condemns both living and dead.
04:06 For this reason also to the dead is proclaimed the gospel, that they might be judged according to men, but living according to God by the Spirit.
04:07 The end has drawn near. Therefore, be self-controlled and alert in your prayers.
04:08 Love one another fervently, for love covers a multitude of sins.
04:09 Hospitality without hypocrisy is to be shown to one another.
04:10 Each according as he has received a gift, let him serve one another, as good stewards of many-colored grace of God.
04:11 Whoever speaks, as if speaking the words of God; whoever serves, as by strength provided by God, so that in all things God may be glorified through Jesus Christ, to whom belong glory and dominion forever and ever. Amen.
04:12 Beloved, do not be surprised at the fiery ordeal that has come upon you to test you, as though something strange were happening to you.
04:13 But rejoice insofar as you share in the sufferings of Christ, that when His glory is revealed, you may also rejoice with exultation.
04:14 If you are insulted because of the name of Christ, blessed (are you), for the Spirit of glory and of God rests on you.
04:15 One of you should not show contempt for authority, but be submissive, for the one in authority is God's servant for your good.
04:16 If indeed as a Christian, let him not be ashamed, but let him glorify God in this name.
04:17 The time of judgment begins with the house of God. What will become of those who do not obey the gospel of God?
04:18 And if the righteous scarcely is saved, where will the unrighteous and sinner appear?
04:19 Those suffering according to the will of God should commit their souls for good works.
05:01 Elderly men among you, I entreat you, the fellow elder and witness of the sufferings of Christ, who also will soon be revealed in glory.
05:02 Shepherd the flock that is among you, which is under your care, not by compulsion but willingly, according to God; not for sordid gain but eagerly.
05:03 Not as overseeing masters, but examples to the flock.
05:04 And when the Chief Shepherd appears, you will receive the imperishable crown of glory.
05:05 Similarly, younger ones, submit yourselves to the elders. All of you clothe yourselves with humility toward one another, because God opposes the proud but gives grace to the humble.
05:06 Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist Him not, that He may lift you up in due time.
05:07 Cast all your anxiety on Him, because He cares for you.
05:08 Awake, be vigilant. Your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, is seeking someone to devour.
05:09 Resist firm in faith, knowing that the same sufferings are being accomplished in your brotherhood that is in the world.
05:10 God of all grace, who called you to his eternal glory in Christ, after you have suffered for a little while, he will make perfect, establish, strengthen, and sustain you.
05:11 His kingdom endures forever and ever. Amen.
05:12 Through Silvanus, to you who are of faith, I consider it right, in a brief writing, to encourage and testify that this is the true grace of God, into which you have been placed.
05:13 Greets you the elect in Babylonia, and Mark, my son.
05:14 Greet one another with a kiss of love. Peace be to all who are in Christ.
