01:01 Paul, an apostle, not from humans nor through human agency, but through Jesus Christ and God the Father, who raised him from the dead.
01:02 And all who are with me, brethren, to the churches of Galatia:
01:03 Grace and peace to you from God the Father and our Lord Jesus Christ.
01:04 Given, who gave Himself for our sins so that He might deliver us from the present evil age according to the will of God and our Father.
01:05 To the ages of the ages, Amen.
01:06 Swiftly transitioning from the grace of Christ to another gospel
01:07 Another gospel is not being preached, if indeed some are disturbing you and trying to pervert the gospel of Christ.
01:08 If even an angel from heaven preaches to you a gospel contrary to what we have preached to you, let him be accursed.
01:09 As we have stated before, and now I say again: If anyone preaches to you a gospel contrary to what you received, let him be accursed.
01:10 People please or God? Do I still please people? If so, I would not be a servant of Christ.
01:11 Announce (I) to you, brethren, the Gospel which I preached, that it is not according to man.
01:12 Received not I from man, nor was I taught, but by the revelation of Jesus Christ.
01:13 You heard of my former life in Judaism, how I persecuted the church of God exceedingly and tried to destroy it.
01:14 I was advancing in Judaism beyond many of my own age among their nation, exceedingly zealous for the traditions of my fathers.
01:15 When God, who had set me apart from my mother's womb and called me by His grace...
01:16 Reveal my Son in me, that I might proclaim Him among the Gentiles. Immediately I did not confer with flesh and blood
01:17 I did not go up to Jerusalem to those who were apostles before me, but I went to Arabia, and again I returned to Damascus.
01:18 After three years I went up to Jerusalem to visit Cephas, and I stayed with him fifteen days.
01:19 Another apostle I did not see, except James, the brother of the Lord.
01:20 I am telling you the truth before God.
01:21 Then I went to the regions of Syria and Cilicia.
01:22 I was unknown by face to the churches of Judea in Christ.
01:23 Now persecutor, now proclaims the faith which once persecuted.
01:24 And glorified in me God.
02:01 After fourteen years, I again went up to Jerusalem with Barnabas, also taking Titus along.
02:02 I was personally revealed to them; I shared the gospel that I preach among the Gentiles, but privately to those who were of repute, lest I should be running in vain or have run in vain.
02:03 Titus, who was with me, was not compelled to be circumcised.
02:04 Despite the false brothers who have infiltrated to examine our freedom that we have in Christ Jesus, so as to enslave us
02:05 We have not submitted to their regime, so that the truth of the Gospel remains with you.
02:06 From those who seem to be something— as though they were something— it makes no difference to me. God shows no favoritism— for those who seemed worthy of nothing, not even I received anything from them.
02:07 But contrastingly, seeing that I believe in the gospel of the uncircumcision as Peter did in the circumcision,
02:08 Worked in Peter for the apostleship of circumcision, worked also in me among the Gentiles.
02:09 Recognizing the grace given to me, Jacob and Cephas and John, who seemed to be pillars, granted me fellowship on an equal level with themselves, so that I might go among the Gentiles, but they for circumcision.
02:10 Remember the poor, for this I also was eager to do.
02:11 When Peter came to Antioch, I opposed him to his face, for he was clearly in the wrong.
02:12 Did eat with the Gentiles before certain people came from James. When he arrived, he began to separate and hold himself aloof, fearing those of circumcision.
02:13 And even Barnabas was carried away with their deceit.
02:14 But when I saw that they were not walking according to the truth of the gospel, I said to Cephas in front of everyone: "If you, being a Jew, live not as a Jew but as one among the Gentiles, why do you compel the Gentiles to Judaize?"
02:15 We Jews by nature are not sinners
02:16 Humans are not justified by the works of the law, but by faith in Jesus Christ. We have believed in Christ Jesus, that we might be justified by faith in Christ and not by the works of the law, for by the works of the law no flesh will be justified.
02:17 If seeking to be justified in Christ we were found also to be sinners, is Christ a minister of sin? Certainly not.
02:18 I am reinforcing what was destroyed, I am affirming myself as a transgressor.
02:19 I have died through the law, that I might live for God; I am crucified with Christ.
02:20 I no longer live, but Christ lives in me; and the life I now live in the flesh I live by faith in the Son of God, who loved me and gave himself for me.
02:21 Do not nullify the grace of God; for if righteousness comes through the law, then Christ died in vain.
03:01 Oh foolish Galatians, who has bewitched you, to whom Jesus Christ was publicly portrayed as crucified?
03:02 From you I desire to learn this alone: Did you receive the Spirit by works of the law, or by hearing of faith?
03:03 Thus, you are foolish. Have you begun in the Spirit, and now are you being made perfect by flesh?
03:04 You have suffered as by a scheme. Even so, by a scheme.
03:05 Who is supplying the spirit and working miracles among you by the works of the law or by hearing of faith?
03:06 Justified is Abraham by faith.
03:07 Children of faith are Abraham.
03:08 Predetermined indeed is the scripture, that from faith God justifies the nations. He announced to Abraham that in you all the nations will be blessed.
03:09 The blessed are those from faith along with the faithful Abraham.
03:10 Condemned are those who are from works of the law, as it is written: "Cursed is everyone who does not continue to do everything written in the book of the law to perform it."
03:11 The righteous shall live by faith.
03:12 The law is not from faith, but the one doing them shall live by them.
03:13 Christ redeemed us from the curse of the law, becoming a curse for us, as it is written: "Cursed is everyone who is hung on a tree."
03:14 In Christ Jesus, the blessing of Abraham might come upon the Gentiles, that we may receive the promise of the Spirit through faith.
03:15 Brothers, I speak as a man: though no one may annul or add to a man's covenant.
03:16 The promises were made to Abraham and to his seed. Not saying, "And to the seeds," as of many, but as of one. And to your Seed, who is Christ.
03:17 This says: "A covenant confirmed before by God remains valid, and the law does not nullify the promise."
03:18 The promise is by virtue of faith in Abraham, God has granted him.
03:19 What is the law? It was added because of transgressions, until the seed to whom the promise was made would come; it was administered through angels by a mediator.
03:20 The mediator of one is not, but God is one.
03:21 The law according to the covenant of God is not; God forbid! If indeed a law were given that could make alive, truly it would have been from law.
03:22 But the scripture shut up all things under sin, that the promise by faith in Jesus Christ might be given to those believing.
03:23 Previously, we were held captive under the law, imprisoned until the coming faith would be revealed.
03:24 The law has become our tutor to lead us to Christ, that we might be justified by faith.
03:25 We are no longer under a tutor (law).
03:26 All are sons of God through faith in Christ Jesus.
03:27 You have been clothed with Christ.
03:28 All of you are one in Christ Jesus.
03:29 If you are Christ's, indeed you are the seed of Abraham, heirs according to promise.
04:01 Child, as long as the heir is a minor, he is no different from a slave—all the same, he is under guardians and managers.
04:02 Under guardians and managers he remains until the will of the father takes effect.
04:03 Thus we also, when we were children, were under the elements of the world.
04:04 When the fullness of time came, God sent forth His Son, born of a woman, born under law.
04:05 So that we might be redeemed from the law, so that we might receive adoption.
04:06 You are sons, God has sent forth the Spirit of His Son into your hearts, crying, "Abba, Father."
04:07 You are no longer a slave, but a son; and if a son, then an heir through God.
04:08 Then, not knowing God, you were enslaved to those who by nature are not gods.
04:09 Now that you know God, or rather are known by God, how can you turn back again to the weak and impoverished elements, whom you want to enslave all over again?
04:10 Days, months, and seasons and years
04:11 Fear not that I have only slightly in vain labored for you.
04:12 Become as I am, for I also have become as you, brethren, beseech you. You have wronged me none.
04:13 You know that through weakness of the flesh I preached to you previously
04:14 You have not despised or rejected my trial in the flesh, but have received me as an angel of God, as Christ Jesus.
04:15 Where then is the blessing you are hoping for? I testify to you that if possible, even eyes you could have plucked out, I would have given them to you.
04:16 I have become your enemy, I am telling you truthfully.
04:17 Desire you not to imitate them falsely, but wish to shut you out, that they may be zealous for you.
04:18 It is good always to be zealous in a good cause, and not only to be present with you.
04:19 My children, for whom I am again in the pains of childbirth until Christ is formed in you
04:20 I desired to be with you now, and to change my tone, because I am in anguish for you.
04:21 Do you, who desire to be under the law, not hear the law?
04:22 Written that Abraham had two sons, one by a slave-girl and one by a free woman.
04:23 Born through a slave-girl naturally, born through a free woman by promise
04:24 These are allegories: They are two covenants; now the one from Mount Sinai was given in bondage, which is Hagar.
04:25 The Mount Sinai is in Arabia; however, Jerusalem now corresponds to it, for she serves with her children.
04:26 The higher Jerusalem is free, which is our mother.
04:27 Mirthful, barren she who has not conceived, tear and cry out, she who does not travail: for the children of the wild are more than those having a husband.
04:28 You are children of Isaac by the covenant.
04:29 Likewise then as the one born by flesh pursued the one born by spirit, so also now.
04:30 But what does the Scripture say? Cast out the slave-girl and her son, for the son of the slave-girl will not inherit with the free son.
04:31 Siblings, we are not slaves' children but of the free woman.
05:01 Christ has set us free; stand fast, and do not be subject again to the yoke of slavery.
05:02 If you are circumcised, Christ will profit you nothing.
05:03 I affirm again to every man who is circumcised that he is debtor to keep the whole law.
05:04 You have fallen from grace.
05:05 We eagerly await through the Spirit from faith the righteousness of hope.
05:06 Faith working through love has no distinction.
05:07 You were running well. Who has hindered you from obeying the truth?
05:08 The persuasion is not from him who is calling you.
05:09 Small leaven leavens the whole lump.
05:10 I have faith in you in the Lord that you will think nothing else; but he who is disturbing you shall bear the judgment, whoever he may be.
05:11 I indeed, brethren, if I still preach circumcision, why do I still suffer persecution? Indeed the offense of the cross has been removed.
05:12 Benefit and those being raised up shall be cut off.
05:13 You were called to freedom, brothers. Only do not use your freedom as an opportunity for self-indulgence, but through love serve one another.
05:14 Love your neighbor as yourself.
05:15 If indeed you bite and devour one another, take heed lest you be consumed by one another.
05:16 Walk by the Spirit, and do not gratify the desires of the flesh.
05:17 The flesh lusts against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh; for these are contrary to one another, so that you do not do what you wish.
05:18 If you are guided by the Spirit, you are not under law.
05:19 Obvious are the works of the flesh, which are fornication, impurity, indecency.
05:20 Idolatry, sorcery, hatred, strife, jealousy, anger, disputes, divisions, heresies
05:21 Envy, drunkenness, revelry, and similar things I am telling you as I have foretold, that those who do such things will not inherit the kingdom of God.
05:22 The fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness,
05:23 Self-control, self-discipline. According to such things, there is no law.
05:24 Those who belong to Christ have crucified the flesh with its passions and desires.
05:25 Live by the Spirit, and do not gratify the desires of the flesh.
05:26 Do not become vain, but exhort one another, and stir up each other to love and good works.
06:01 Fellow believers, if someone is caught in a sin, you who are spiritual should restore him gently, looking to yourself, so that you also may not be tempted.
06:02 Carry one another's burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ.
06:03 If anyone thinks he can be something without being subject to the law, that man is deceiving himself.
06:04 Each should examine his own work, and then he will have reason for boasting, not against another.
06:05 Each shall bear his own load.
06:06 The one being instructed should share in all good things with the one doing the instructing.
06:07 Do not be deceived, God does not be mocked; for whatever a man sows, that he will also reap.
06:08 Sowing to the flesh results in corruption, but sowing to the Spirit produces eternal life.
06:09 Reaping what is good, we do not become discouraged, for at the right time we will reap, if we do not give up.
06:10 Therefore, as opportunity comes, we work for the good of all, but especially for those related to the faith.
06:11 You have been written about in large letters with my own hand.
06:12 Those desiring to appear in the flesh compel you to be circumcised, only that in the cross of Christ they may not be persecuted.
06:13 Uncircumcised men, in fact, do not keep the law; rather, they want you to be circumcised so that they may boast against you in your flesh.
06:14 It is not I who am boasting, except in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, through whom the world has been crucified to me, and I to the world.
06:15 New creation.
06:16 And to those who are under the law, peace and mercy, and to the Israelites of God.
06:17 I bear in my body the marks of Jesus.
06:18 Grace be to you and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ, brethren. Amen.
