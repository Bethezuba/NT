01:01 What was from the beginning, what we have heard, what we have seen with our eyes, what we beheld and our hands have touched concerning the Word of life
01:02 And the life appeared, and we have seen and testify and declare to you the eternal life which was with the Father and was made manifest to us.
01:03 We have seen, heard, and are telling you so that you may also have fellowship with us. And our fellowship is with the Father and with His Son Jesus Christ.
01:04 And these things we write to you that our joy may be made complete.
01:05 This is the message we have heard from him and proclaim to you: God is light, and in him there is no darkness at all.
01:06 If we say that we have fellowship with him and walk in darkness, we lie and do not practice the truth.
01:07 If we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship with one another, and the blood of Jesus his Son cleanses us from all sin.
01:08 If we say that we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us.
01:09 If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.
01:10 If we say that we have no sin, we are deceiving ourselves and the truth is not in us.
02:01 My dear children, I write this to you so that you may not sin. And if anyone does sin, we have an advocate with the Father, Jesus Christ the righteous.
02:02 And He Himself is the propitiation for our sins, and not for ours only but also for the whole world.
02:03 In this, we know that we have known Him, if we keep His commandments.
02:04 Liars do not know Him nor His commandments. And in this, the truth is not in them.
02:05 The one who keeps His word, in this love of God is perfected. In this, we know that we are in Him.
02:06 Whoever says they abide in Him ought to walk as He walked.
02:07 Beloved, no new commandment I write to you, but an old one which you had from the beginning. The old commandment is the word which you have heard.
02:08 Again, I write to you a new commandment, which is true in Him and in you: that the darkness is passing away and the true Light is already appearing.
02:09 Loves in the light and hates his brother remains in darkness until now.
02:10 Loves in the light abides, and there is no occasion for stumbling in him.
02:11 Hates his brother in darkness resides, and walks in darkness, and does not know where he is going, because darkness has blinded his eyes.
02:12 Written to you, children, that your sins are forgiven through His name.
02:13 I write to you, fathers, that you have known Him who is from the beginning. I write to you, young men, that you have overcome the wicked one.
02:14 You wrote to children, that you have known the Father. You wrote to fathers, that you have known Him who is from the beginning. You wrote to young men, that you are strong and the word of God remains in you, and you have overcome the wicked one.
02:15 Do not love the world nor the things in the world. Whoever loves the world does not have the love of the Father in him.
02:16 "For all that is in the world, the desire of the flesh and the desire of the eyes and the pride of life, is not from the Father, but is from the world.
02:17 The world is passing away, and also its desire; but he who does the will of God abides forever.
02:18 The little children, it is the last hour; and as you have heard that the Antichrist is coming, even now many antichrists have come. Therefore we know that it is the last hour.
02:19 They went out from us, but they were not of us; for if they had been of us, they would have remained with us; but that it may be evident that they are not all of us.
02:20 You have received the Holy Spirit, and you all know.
02:21 You have not written to you that you do not know the truth, but that you do know it, and that any lie is not from the truth.
02:22 The liar is the one who denies that Jesus is the Christ. This one is the antichrist, the one who denies the Father and the Son.
02:23 Everyone who denies the Son has not the Father; he who acknowledges the Son has the Father.
02:24 You have heard from the beginning, let it remain in you. If what you have heard from the beginning remains in you, you also will remain in the Son and in the Father.
02:25 And this is the promise that He Himself made to us, eternal life.
02:26 Wrote these things to you concerning the deceivers.
02:27 And the anointing which you received from Him remains in you, and you do not need anyone to teach you; but as His anointing teaches you concerning all things, and is true and is not a lie, and just as He taught you, remain in Him.
02:28 Remain in Him, now, so that when He is revealed we may have confidence and not be ashamed before Him at His coming.
02:29 If you know that He is righteous, you know that everyone who practices righteousness is born of Him.
03:01 The Father has given us this world's kind of love, that we should love one another. That is how people know we are His disciples. Therefore, the world does not know us because it did not know Him.
03:02 Beloved, now we are children of God, yet what we shall be has not yet appeared; but we know that when He appears, we shall be like Him, for we shall see Him as He is.
03:03 And everyone who hopes in him purifies himself, just as He is pure.
03:04 Committing sin is lawlessness.
03:05 That one was manifested that he might take away sins, and in him is no sin.
03:06 Everyone who abides in Him does not sin; everyone who sins has not seen or known Him.
03:07 Children, do not be led astray. He who does righteousness is righteous, as he is righteous.
03:08 The one doing sin is of the devil, because from the beginning the devil has been sinning. For this purpose the Son of God was manifested, that He might undo the works of the devil.
03:09 Everyone born of God does not sin, because his seed remains in him; and he cannot sin, because he is born of God.
03:10 In this way the children of God are manifest, and the children of the devil. Anyone who does not practice righteousness is not of God, and anyone who does not love his brother.
03:11 This is the message you have heard from the beginning: that we should love one another.
03:12 Not like Cain who was from the evil one and murdered his brother. And why did he murder him? Because his works were evil, but his brother's were righteous.
03:13 Does not marvel, brethren, if the world hates you.
03:14 We know that we have passed from death to life, because we love one another. He who does not love remains in death.
03:15 Everyone who hates his brother is a murderer, and you know that any murderer does not have eternal life abiding in him.
03:16 In this, we have come to know love, because He gave His life for us. We also ought to give our lives for the brothers.
03:17 Whoever has the world's goods and sees his brother in need and closes his heart from him, how does God's love remain in him?
03:18 Love not in words or speech, but in action and truth.
03:19 In this we will be known that we are from the truth, and before Him we will convince our hearts.
03:20 If anyone knows us, it is that God is greater than our hearts and understands all.
03:21 Beloved, if our hearts do not condemn us, we have confidence toward God.
03:22 And whatever we ask, we receive from Him, because we keep His commandments and do what is pleasing to Him.
03:23 This is His commandment, that we believe in the name of His Son Jesus Christ and love one another, as He commanded us.
03:24 He who keeps His commands dwells in Him, and He in him. And this is how we know that He dwells in us: by the Spirit whom He gave us.
04:01 Beloved, do not believe every spirit, but test the spirits to see if they are from God, for many false prophets have gone out into the world.
04:02 In this, you know the spirit of God: every spirit that confesses Jesus Christ has come in flesh from God is from Him.
04:03 And every spirit that does not confess Jesus Christ has not come from God; this is the antichrist, which you have heard is coming, and now it is already in the world.
04:04 You are from God, children, and you have overcome them, because He who is in you is greater than he who is in the world.
04:05 They are from the world, therefore they speak of the world, and the world listens to them.
04:06 We are from God; he who knows God listens to us, and whoever is not from God does not listen to us. From this we know the Spirit of truth and the spirit of error.
04:07 Beloved, let us love one another, for love comes from God, and whoever loves has been born of God and knows God.
04:08 He who does not love does not know God, for God is love.
04:09 In this, God's love was revealed in us, that God sent His one and only Son into the world so that we might live through Him.
04:10 In this, love is manifested, not that we loved God, but that He loved us and sent His Son as a propitiation for our sins.
04:11 Beloved, if God loved us, we ought to love one another.
04:12 God has never been seen; if we love one another, God dwells in us, and his love is perfected in us.
04:13 In this we know that we abide in him, and he in us, because he has given us of his Spirit.
04:14 And we have seen and testify that the Father sent the Son, who is Savior of the world.
04:15 If anyone confesses that Jesus is the Son of God, God dwells in him, and he in God.
04:16 And we have known and believed the love which God has in us. God is love, and he who abides in love abides in God, and God in him.
04:17 In this way, love is made complete among us, so that we may have confidence for the day of judgment; because as He is, so are we in this world.
04:18 Fear has not been produced in love; but the perfect love casts out fear, because fear involves punishment, and the one who fears is not perfected in love.
04:19 We love because He first loved us.
04:20 The one who says they love God and hates their brother or sister, is a liar; for the one who does not love their brother or sister whom they have seen, cannot love God whom they have not seen.
04:21 And this command we have from Him, that he who loves God loves his brother also.
05:01 Everyone who believes that Jesus is the Christ, who is born of God, loves also him who begot Him.
05:02 In this we know that we love the children of God, when we love God and keep His commandments.
05:03 This is the love of God, that we keep His commandments; and His commandments are not burdensome.
05:04 This is the victory that overcomes the world: our faith.
05:05 Who is it that conquers the world, if not he who believes that Jesus is the Son of God?
05:06 This is the one who came by water and blood, Jesus Christ, not by water alone, but by water and blood. And it is the Spirit who bears witness, because the Spirit is truth.
05:07 The three testify
05:08 The Spirit, the water, and the blood; and these three are one.
05:09 The testimony of men is inferior, for the testimony of God is greater; and this is the testimony of God, that He has testified concerning His Son.
05:10 The one believing in the Son of God has the testimony in himself; the one not believing God made him a liar, because he did not believe in the testimony that God testified concerning His Son.
05:11 And this is the testimony, that God gave us eternal life, and this is the life which is in His Son.
05:12 The one who has the Son has life; the one who does not have the Son of God does not have life.
05:13 You have eternal life, to those who believe in the name of the Son of God.
05:14 And this is the confidence that we have toward him, that if we ask according to his will, he hears us.
05:15 We know that he hears us whenever we ask of him, because we know that we have the requests which we have asked from him.
05:16 If a brother or sister sins, it is not fatal—one should ask, and God will grant life; the sin that leads to death does not need to be asked about. There is sin leading to death; I am not saying that he should ask about that.
05:17 All sin is transgression, and transgression is sin.
05:18 Whoever is born of God does not sin, for God's nature abides in them, and they cannot sin because God's nature is within them; evil cannot touch them.
05:19 We know that we are from God, and the whole world lies in the evil one.
05:20 We know that the Son of God has come, and He has given us understanding so that we may know the truth; and we are in the truth, in the Son of God, Jesus Christ. This is the true God and eternal life.
05:21 Children, keep yourselves from idols.
