01:01 Dear Gaius, whom I love in truth.
01:02 Beloved, I wish all things to be well with you and health, as it goes well with your soul.
01:03 I rejoice greatly in those who confirm your truth, as you walk in truth.
01:04 Greater joy I do not have, than to hear that my children walk in truth.
01:05 Beloved, you are doing what is worthy in whatever way you aid the brothers, and especially those who are strangers.
01:06 Those who testify to your love before the church, well may you send them worthy of God.
01:07 For we received nothing from non-Christians.
01:08 We ought to support such people, so that we may become coworkers in truth.
01:09 Diotrephes does not receive us.
01:10 Through this, if I come, I will call attention to his deeds, the harmful words he speaks against us, and not being content with these things, neither does he welcome brothers nor allows those who wish, and from the church expels them.
01:11 Dearly loved, do not imitate evil but what is good. The one who does what is good comes from God. The one who does what is evil has not seen God.
01:12 Demetrius is testified by all and by herself the truth, and we also testify, and you know that our testimony is true.
01:13 I had many things to write to you, but I do not wish to do so with ink and pen.
01:14 I expect to see you soon, and we will speak face to face.
01:15 Peace to you. The friends greet you. Greet the friends by name.
